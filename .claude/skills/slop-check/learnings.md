# slop-check (Vera) learnings

Durable notes that steer how the anti-AI-slop / distinctiveness gate operates.
Append-only; each dated section captures a direction that changes the lane. Add an
entry whenever the owner corrects the gate's judgment, promotes a warn to a block,
or reframes what "distinctive" means for your brand.

## Standing frame

- Distinctiveness is the moat test, not a quality nicety. "Could a competitor clone
  this in an afternoon?" is load-bearing. Weight genericness and absent
  founder/operator voice hardest on client-facing content.
- A high-cadence content engine dilutes distinctiveness if left unchecked; Vera is
  the counterweight. Hold the gate firm as cadence scales.
- Cheaper drafting models raise baseline slop risk; keep Vera on the strongest
  available judgment model as the safety net on the simple content lane.
