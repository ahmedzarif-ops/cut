## Proposed refinements - HUMAN REVIEW before applying
- **Content-craft patterns (ratify before making canonical):**
  - **ATOMIZE, DON'T AUTHOR** (hub-and-spoke): one canonical voice source (the owner's captured thinking —
    the ICP research, message spine, and market-radar feed) is the wellspring; the engine
    REGROUPS/REFORMATS those exact takes per platform, NEVER invents a new opinion or story. This is both a
    voice-consistency moat and an anti-slop guardrail on high-volume output.
  - **PROOF → PROMISE → PLAN opener**: lead with a real on-screen artifact, not a curiosity-gap tease.
    Packaging: titles/thumbnails go vague→clear; curiosity-gap banned.
  - **CONTENT IS THE TARGETING**: hard-narrow single-segment concepts (one persona/vertical at a time);
    demote raw views; judge by qualified-signup / conversation rate, not impressions.
  - **Exceptional-asset checklist**: hook resolves in ~3s · one variable per iteration · score + refine the
    thumbnail and score the title before publish · split-test top thumbnails.
  - **Volume you can SUSTAIN**: weight formats the creator actually likes (Q&A, real build sessions,
    decision walkthroughs) so cadence is durable, not a grind.
