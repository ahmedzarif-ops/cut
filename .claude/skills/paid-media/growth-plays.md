# Growth Plays

The growth-OS layer. These are cross-channel plays the `growth-strategist` selects from
when the brief asks for more than paid ads: creative lead-gen, channel expansion,
partnerships, referral, and lifecycle. Paid plays execute inside this engine; organic
and lifecycle plays ROUTE to the engine that already runs them.

## How to use this file

- Recommend a play only when its audience, offer, and effort fit the brief. Tie each
  recommendation to the honesty separation (Hypothesis vs Recommendation requiring
  testing) and a measurement plan.
- "Executes via" tells you who runs it: this skill (paid), `blog-engine` (blog/SEO),
  `social-engine` (organic social), `email-marketing` (lifecycle email), any in-product
  follow-up capability, or the owner (outbound / partnerships / fulfillment). Do not
  re-implement an organic engine here.
- Plays are grounded in your ICP pains (ICP research doc) and your existing assets.
  Mark untested ideas as Hypothesis.

## Plays (adapt the specifics to your ICP)

### Free diagnostic as the cold lead magnet
- What it is: lead with the free diagnostic ("send me your [site/situation] and I will
  show you why it is not [getting the outcome]") as the value-first cold hook.
- Tier / offer: entry (free diagnostic -> entry paid offer).
- Channel: paid social, Reddit, TikTok/Reels, organic social.
- Effort: low (once the asset exists and is live).
- When to use: almost every cold push; it is the wedge that creates the "here is proof"
  moment.
- Executes via: this skill (paid) + social-engine (organic).

### Leak-diagnostic teaser
- What it is: a carousel or short video walking the places the outcome leaks before the
  buyer converts, ending at the diagnostic.
- Tier / offer: entry (free diagnostic).
- Channel: paid-social carousel, Reels, video.
- Effort: low to medium.
- When to use: Problem-Aware cold traffic that needs the leak named.
- Executes via: this skill (paid creative) + social-engine (organic repurpose).

### Follow-up rescue story
- What it is: the "the [proposal] you sent may not be dead, just unfollowed" angle
  showing a follow-up sequence recovering a stalled deal (sample data, clearly labeled).
- Tier / offer: recurring offer + any in-product follow-up capability.
- Channel: paid social, video, email nurture.
- Effort: medium.
- When to use: high-ticket / long-consideration segments.
- Executes via: this skill (paid) + the product follow-up capability (the live proof).

### Alternative-to-incumbent content + retargeting
- What it is: "stop renting reach" content (blog + comparison page) amplified by paid
  and used as a retargeting destination for incumbent-fatigued buyers.
- Tier / offer: entry -> recurring.
- Channel: search (alternative queries), blog, retargeting.
- Effort: medium.
- When to use: buyers paying incumbent fees; high-intent search capture.
- Executes via: blog-engine (the content) + this skill (paid amplification +
  retargeting).

### Partnership and co-marketing
- What it is: a co-branded diagnostic or guide with a partner who shares the buyer
  audience and does not compete. Near-zero CAC.
- Tier / offer: entry + recurring.
- Channel: partnership / co-marketing.
- Effort: medium to high (relationship-driven).
- When to use: when a partner with the same audience and no conflict is available.
- Executes via: the owner (relationship) + this skill (the co-branded asset and offer).

### Referral loop for existing customers
- What it is: a structured referral ask to current recurring customers, timed to a win.
- Tier / offer: retention + new entry/recurring acquisition.
- Channel: lifecycle email / in-product.
- Effort: low once a customer base exists.
- When to use: after the first cohort of paying customers exists (Hypothesis until
  then).
- Executes via: email-marketing / in-product (lifecycle) + the owner.

### Community answer-the-question presence
- What it is: founder-led helpful answers in the buyer's communities (Reddit, niche
  forums, local networks), seeding the diagnostic where it genuinely helps.
- Tier / offer: entry.
- Channel: organic community (with a small forum-ads test where traction shows).
- Effort: medium (ongoing presence).
- When to use: communities where your buyer already gathers.
- Executes via: social-engine (organic) + the owner (founder voice) + this skill (ads
  test).

### Founder-credibility + demo video
- What it is: founder explainer and screen-recorded product demos that warm a skeptical
  buyer, used as both organic content and paid pre-roll, then retargeted.
- Tier / offer: recurring / high-touch.
- Channel: video (organic + paid) + retargeting.
- Effort: medium to high (video production).
- When to use: Product-Aware buyers and high-touch prospects who need to trust first.
- Executes via: social-engine / your video pipeline + this skill (paid pre-roll).

### Association / business-group ABM (high-touch)
- What it is: targeted outbound-supported account-based plays to relevant associations
  and larger operators for the high-touch offer.
- Tier / offer: high-touch.
- Channel: professional network + outbound + warm retargeting.
- Effort: high.
- When to use: larger buyers worth a high-touch motion.
- Executes via: the owner (outbound) + this skill (professional network + retargeting).

### Webinar / live-sample demo
- What it is: a webinar plus a live sample of the product as a mid-funnel conversion
  event for Solution/Product-Aware buyers.
- Tier / offer: recurring / high-touch.
- Channel: webinar (paid registration + organic + email).
- Effort: medium (once the asset exists).
- When to use: warming buyers toward a subscription/consult.
- Executes via: the owner (host) + this skill (paid registration) + social-engine
  (promo).

### Live workshop / readiness diagnostic (conversion event)
- What it is: a free->paid conversion event. Teach a full framework, diagnose the
  viewer's fit, show real proof, THEN offer the paid path — never withhold the
  framework to force the pitch.
- Tier / offer: free tier -> the paid program.
- Channel: live workshop (registration) + video + email.
- Effort: medium.
- When to use: as a standing conversion event once enough content exists.
- Executes via: the owner (host) + social-engine (promo) + email-marketing (invite
  flow).

### Flagship acquisition proof-film (top-of-funnel asset)
- What it is: the strongest cold-acquisition piece — a proof-film that shows the real
  system doing the work on screen. Assembles from retention-proven segments.
- Tier / offer: top of funnel (free entry).
- Channel: video flagship lane + paid amplification once instrumented.
- Effort: high (production).
- When to use: as the channel's flagship proof anchor.
- Executes via: the video pipeline + this skill (paid amplification).

### Seasonal-trigger campaigns
- What it is: time campaigns to your market's real seasonal demand using honest
  seasonal urgency.
- Tier / offer: entry / recurring.
- Channel: paid social + search.
- Effort: low (scheduling discipline).
- When to use: ahead of each real seasonal peak (see `segmentation.md`).
- Executes via: this skill (paid) + blog-engine (seasonal content).
