## What works (durable patterns)
- Retention = "does the customer keep getting real value?" Measure value-producing activity, not login
  frequency: an account can be quiet on logins yet still producing value (or busy on logins yet
  producing none). Anchor weekly-health + at-risk on outcomes, never on usage clicks.
- Lead every customer-facing value readout with the concrete outcome the product produced for them.
- Concierge onboarding for the earliest accounts: guarantee each a visible week-1 value win. That seeds
  the founder-led flywheel and feeds advocacy-readiness (advocacy only after a verified win + consent).
